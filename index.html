    About Manual Humanizer

    Manual Humanizer is a free, browser-based tool that helps writers, students, marketers, and developers rewrite AI-generated or robotic-sounding text into natural, human-sounding prose. Instead of pressing one magic button, it gives you a guided, sentence-by-sentence workflow so the final text actually reads like you — because you reviewed every line.

    Paste your full draft once, and Manual Humanizer automatically detects paragraphs and sentences. You then refine each sentence in turn, with a built-in Datamuse-powered thesaurus that lets you click any word to see and apply synonyms instantly. When you want a faster rewrite, the optional Tweak with AI button uses your own OpenAI API key to generate several humanized variants of the current sentence — picking the best one is one click away.

    Key features

    • Sentence-by-sentence editor with paragraph structure preserved.
    • Click-to-replace thesaurus & synonym lookup (no signup, free API).
    • Optional OpenAI-powered humanization with multiple tone presets.
    • Auto-save to browser cache so refreshing won't lose your progress.
    • Light & dark themes, keyboard shortcuts, copy or download as .txt.
    • Runs 100% in your browser. Your text never goes to a third-party server (except per-sentence calls to the AI you explicitly trigger).

    Frequently asked questions

    Is Manual Humanizer free?

    Yes. The editor, thesaurus, copy, and download features are 100% free. The optional AI tweak uses your own OpenAI API key, so you only pay OpenAI for what you use.

    Does it send my text anywhere?

    Your text stays in your browser. Synonym lookups send only individual words to the public Datamuse API. The AI tweak sends only the current sentence to OpenAI, and only when you click the button.

    How does the AI tweak feature work?

    Open Settings, paste your OpenAI API key (stored only in your browser), pick a tone and number of variants, then click Tweak with AI on any sentence. The tool returns several human-sounding variants you can apply with one click.

    Will I lose my work on refresh?

    No. Every edit is saved to localStorage automatically. Reload the page and everything — your full draft, every sentence edit, your current position, and the final output — is restored.

    Who is this tool for?

    Anyone who wants AI-assisted text to read naturally: writers, content creators, marketers, students, non-native speakers polishing English drafts, and developers cleaning up generated documentation.
